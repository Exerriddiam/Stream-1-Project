About the Texas Ticks

In 2017 two good friends from the Deep South found themselves magically transported to the heart of the English Cotswolds with nothing but guitar and a banjo for company.

Determined to make the best of their plight they pledged an oath to one another....To bring tales of loss, love, beards, moonshine and reptiles to a new audience.

And so Texas Tick Fever were born, Featuring Doctor Slash on vocals and an ever increasing assortment of guitars and Reverend Stretch on vocals, guitar and banjo with a little kickdrum for good measure. Available for hoedowns, square dances. barn raisin and general good times.

We are a Americana/Blues playing style kinda Texas duo, Please look at our videos and Pictures.
